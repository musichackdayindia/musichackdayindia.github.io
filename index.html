Music Hack Day India – 2025

Music Hack Day India is a gathering of researchers, developers, creative coders, musicians, makers, engineers, audiophiles and more. We are excited to bring you another fun hackathon scheduled for 17th & 18th Jan, 2025.

The hack day will be followed by the ADCx India main event on 19th Jan 2025.

Call for Talks for ADCx India 25 is live. You can apply here:

Submit a Talk to ADCx India 25

ADCx India is a three day meet-up for audio developers combining Music Hack Day India and a one-day Audio Developer Conference pop-up. We are also providing Diversity Grants to support talents.

As part of bringing the audio tech community together, we have setup the Music Tech Community's Discord server. Join us there.
